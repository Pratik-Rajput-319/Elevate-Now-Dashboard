Project Overview

Designed an interactive Power BI Executive Dashboard for a healthcare and weight-management company (Elevate Now). The dashboard enables management to monitor business performance, patient engagement, marketing effectiveness, and customer retention through data-driven insights.

Some Key points which can be noticed:-

Tracked monthly revenue trends to identify business performance.

Analyzed primary reasons for customer churn.

Compared marketing channels based on member acquisition and revenue generation.

Evaluated member distribution across age groups and subscription plans.

Monitored average weight loss achieved under different subscription plans.
